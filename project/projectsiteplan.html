MediTrack Site Plan

Site Name

MediTrack - Hospital Management System

The name "MediTrack" was selected to convey the system's primary purpose of efficiently tracking and managing medical information and workflows. The combination of "Medi" (for medical) and "Track" suggests a platform that monitors, organizes, and follows medical processes - exactly what our hospital management system aims to do. The name is concise, memorable, and clearly communicates the healthcare focus of the application.

Potential domain: https://emalcom.github.io/wdd231/project/projectsiteplan.html.com

Site Purpose

MediTrack serves as a comprehensive web-based hospital management system that streamlines healthcare administrative workflows through four key components:

  1. Patient Management: Providing secure registration, profile management, and search capabilities for patient information.
  2. Appointment Scheduling: Offering interactive calendars for booking appointments with availability checking and automated reminders.
  3. Medical Records: Enabling secure storage and retrieval of patient medical histories, documentation of treatments, and lab result tracking.
  4. Administrative Dashboard: Delivering real-time analytics on patient flow, staff scheduling tools, and resource allocation monitoring.

The platform aims to improve healthcare efficiency, reduce administrative overhead, minimize record-keeping errors, and enhance patient care through better information management, all while maintaining data security and privacy standards.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Front Desk Administrator

"I need to register a new patient who just arrived at the hospital. How can I quickly input their information and generate a patient ID without duplicating existing records?"

Scenario 2: Doctor

"I have a patient consultation in 10 minutes. Where can I efficiently access their complete medical history, including previous diagnoses, treatments, and recent lab results?"

Scenario 3: Hospital Manager

"Our emergency department seems to be experiencing longer wait times this month. How can I view analytics to identify bottlenecks in patient processing and optimize resource allocation?"

Scenario 4: Nurse

"A patient needs to schedule a follow-up appointment with their specialist next week. How can I check doctor availability across multiple departments and book the appointment efficiently?"

Primary Buttons Healing Green (#38A169) Draws attention to primary actions
Critical Alerts Alert Red (#E53E3E) Immediately identifies urgent matters
Section Headers Medical Blue (#2C5282) Maintains brand consistency
Secondary Text Calm Gray (#718096) Provides visual hierarchy for less important information
